How It Works

Complete compliance automation from generation to filing

Ready-to-File Regulatory Reports

Our system automatically generates ready-to-file regulatory reports including statutory forms like SH-6 (Return of Allotment), PAS-3 (Return of Buyback/Reduction of Share Capital), and other mandatory statutory disclosures. All reports are pre-formatted and aligned with regulatory requirements.

  • Form SH-6
  • Form PAS-3
  • Statutory disclosures

Legal & Regulatory Alignment

Every filing is ensured to comply with the Companies Act, SEBI guidelines, and other statutory regulations. Our built-in compliance engine validates all data against current regulatory requirements before report generation.

  • Companies Act
  • SEBI guidelines
  • Statutory regulations

Audit-Ready Documentation

Maintain complete audit readiness with traceable logs, comprehensive version control, and detailed filing histories for all regulatory submissions. Every report generation and filing is logged with timestamps and user tracking.

  • Traceable logs
  • Version control
  • Filing histories
