Who We Are

ESOP Guardian is a specialized ESOP advisory and ESOP management platform offering end-to-end, 360° ESOP solutions across the entire employee equity lifecycle—right from ESOP strategy, scheme design, valuation, and regulatory compliance to digital ESOP execution, employee engagement, and ongoing ESOP administration.

We simplify ESOPs while amplifying employee value, by enabling organizations to use equity-based compensation and employee stock option plans as a powerful tool for business growth, talent retention, and long-term wealth creation.

At ESOP Guardian, we address practical challenges faced by companies, from startup to large companies, in implementing ESOPs—complex legal and tax frameworks, valuation accuracy, SEBI compliance, employee awareness, ESOP automation, and liquidity planning. Our multidisciplinary team of Company Secretaries, Chartered Accountants, Valuation Experts, Legal Advisors, IT Professionals, and MBAs works in close coordination to deliver customized ESOP, RSU, SAR, and Phantom Stock programs that are regulatory-ready, tax-efficient, and strategically aligned with business objectives.

Our key differentiator is our technology-integrated ESOP management platform, which automates the complete ESOP lifecycle management—from grant and vesting to exercise, accounting, taxation, and statutory reporting. Real-time dashboards, vesting alerts, and personalized employee views enhance transparency and help employees visualize long-term equity wealth creation, fostering trust and an ownership mindset.

Beyond advisory and technology, we actively partner with clients through employee ESOP education programs, management workshops, and dedicated relationship support to ensure smooth execution and high adoption.

What is an ESOP and how does it work?

An Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) allows employees to gain ownership in the company through stock options. After a vesting period, employees can buy shares at a set price, aligning their interests with the company's growth and aiding in talent retention.

How quickly can I set up an ESOP scheme with ESOP Guardian?

Our streamlined platform is designed for fast and seamless onboarding. Depending on the complexity of your company's structure, setup can typically be completed within a few days to a week, much faster than traditional methods.

Is ESOP Guardian compliant with Indian regulations?

Yes, absolutely. ESOP Guardian is meticulously designed to comply with the Companies Act, 2013, and all relevant SEBI/RBI guidelines for ESOPs in India. We provide automated compliance checks and reporting to keep you worry-free.
